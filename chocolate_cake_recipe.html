Recipe

Chocolate cake

"Here is a fast and easy version of the classic chocolate cake recipe. It is fast, amazing and your kids will love it."

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients:

Black chocolate: 200g

Sugar: 150g

Butter: 200g

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80g

Instructions:

1

 Add the boiling water to the mixture.

2

 Remove the cakes from the oven.

3

 Spread a little chocolate icing.

4

 Transfer the cake to a serving plate.
